Fire Emblem Scared Me

No it really did. But hey it's actually pretty fun.

As I grew more confident in my gaming skills I wanted to reach out and try new games. One of the games that I kept noticing was called Fire Emblem. It was somewhat similar to Pokemon and it semmed like I would be good at it. It tempeted me for many years and yet I refused to get it. The reason was because it scared me. One of the core features of the game is perma-death. This means that if your character dies they die forever. I don't know why but this made me very nervous as a kid. I didn't like to think that my units would die and never come back. I didn't get the game for a long time. Finally my curiosity grew over the fear and i got the game. It turned out to be one of my most favorite games of all time. I started out by playing the game called Fire Emblem Awakening.

Lucky for me the game was later in the series. This meant that there were multiple settings. You could turn perma-death off. Hooray. I found that I was really bad at the game. Like Pokemon I just trained up the base units. I've come back to play this game many more times even with perma-death on. I have gotten much better strategy wise. the turn based grid combat system was a bit strange for me to learn but I loved it. I continue to play Fire Emblem today and plan to continue. Their games seem to be getting better and better (unlike Pokemon) so I look forward to their next release.
